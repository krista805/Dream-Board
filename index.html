What is a dream board?

A dream board (or vision board) is a visual tool that serves as a guide to your goals for the future.[1] It is a visual representation of your dreams and your ideal life.[2] In addition, some people believe that a dream board “activates” the Law of Attraction (which states that you can program your mindset to actually attract things and situations into your life).[3] Making your own unique dream board can be a chance to explore your own goals and dreams and exercise your creativity.
